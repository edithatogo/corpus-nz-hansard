# Licensing and Provenance

## Source Material

The source artifact in this workspace is:

- `2024-09-06 Hansard Extract from DocumentsDB.zip`

It contains New Zealand Parliament Hansard CSV extracts for Parliament numbers 47 through 54.

## Official Hansard Status

New Zealand Parliament's Parliamentary Practice chapter states that:

- Hansard is the official report of debates in the House.
- Hansard is published on the Parliament website and as New Zealand Parliamentary Debates.
- Digitised copies of NZPD volumes are available through the Parliament website.
- No copyright exists in Hansard / New Zealand Parliamentary Debates.

Official source:

- `https://www3.parliament.nz/en/visit-and-learn/how-parliament-works/parliamentary-practice-in-new-zealand-2023-by-chapter/chapter-12-communicating-and-engaging-with-parliament/`

Relevant sections:

- 12.3 Hansard
- 12.10 Copyright, licences, and terms of use
