# Spec: Full Historical Sitting Reconciliation

## Goal

Move from supplied-archive coverage auditing to official full historical sitting/proceeding reconciliation before completeness claims.

## MoSCoW Requirements

### Must

- Identify official sitting/proceeding inventories and compare them against corpus holdings.
- Report missing, duplicate, partial, malformed, and source-unavailable records.
- Separate archive coverage from true historical completeness.

### Should

- Generate remediation lists and acquisition priorities.
- Feed sitting/proceeding component and documentation portal tracks.

### Could

- Add visual coverage timelines.

### Won't

- Claim complete Hansard coverage from available archive files alone.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Reconciliation report, manifest, docs, and gap list exist.
- Public documentation uses reconciliation-backed completeness language only.
